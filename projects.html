Simple.css

Version 2.1


Simple.css is a CSS framework that creates a modern design to webpages.

Completly open sourced and used on this website!

Lime Classes

Version 0.1 (alpha)


Lime Classes is a learning management system created for the students.
It was created with privacy and poor to no internet connection in mind.
It enables students to turn in their work offline and even edit offline and when they get connected again it will submit to the teacher.
