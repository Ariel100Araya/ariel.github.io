Beta Program


Want to test new pre release projects?


The Beta Program lets you test new projects and give me feedback on them via SMS and iMessage. Keep in mind that not everyone gets accepted into the program and once the testing period is over, you have to give the product back.

How do I sign up?


Click the link here. Your request should contain your name, your computer platform (e.g Windows 11, iOS 15, macOS 12, macOS Monterey, Android 12, Samsung OneUI, etc), how you can be contacted (Phone number/E-Mail), and at the end of the message say "BetaProgram 1101".

How do I know I am in the beta program?


You will get an e-mail/message in a few hours with an acceptance and contact info.
