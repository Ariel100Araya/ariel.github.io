What is Lime USB?


Lime USB is a USB stick that can boot a full version of Windows 10 or Windows 8.1.


Spend too much time syncing your work computer with your personal one? Or you spend all day in meetings and need to present a Presentation? Well then, this is for you!


All your apps, files, and everything else, just connect it!


Your computer is your home, you know it from front to back, so why learn new layouts? When you connect your USB, you get booted to the same Desktop, same Files, same Apps, everything right where you left it. Because it is your computer, it is the same Windows, nothing changed.


No more It doesn't open moments


Lets say you have a PowerPoint presentation ready, you've been working on it for all night and your ready to present it to the class. If PowerPoint doesn't open on their computer, it makes everything more unproductive, while if you use our USB, you can connect it and run everything with your apps, just like you expected.


Security is number one priority, now and always


Computers from work, schools, and public places have bloatware and spyware, they have keyboard loggers that log your passwords, adware that sells your activity to Google and Meta and more unsafe programs. When you use your USB, the computer doesn't turn on the hard drive but instead uses the USB storage to load your desktop, so those programs doesn't run.
